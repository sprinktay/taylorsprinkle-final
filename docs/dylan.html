dylan brady.

Brady produces and records music under his own name, and has his own label, Dog Show Records; both 100 Gecs and Dog Show have been signed with Mad Decent's Decent Distribution since late 2018. He became interested in music after being part of his high school choir and studied acoustic engineering in college for three years before moving to Los Angeles.[21] Brady has said 100 Gecs' style is influenced by Breathe Carolina, John Zorn, and I See Stars, among others.
